# Stella - Worklog

## Project Snapshot

- Brand name: `Stella`
- Positioning: premium Italian agricultural brand focused on traceable Puglian almonds
- Channels: direct ecommerce plus B2B requests
- Product focus today:
  - almonds in shell
  - natural shelled almonds
  - blanched almonds
  - almond flour
  - almond nibs

## Work Done

### Functional Direction

- Defined Stella as a premium, essential, non-generic agricultural brand.
- Clarified that the ecommerce should feel closer to high-end wine or olive oil than to a generic food catalog.
- Defined two customer paths:
  - private customers can buy with guest checkout or optional account creation
  - business customers can request dedicated supply terms for larger volumes

### Backoffice

- Produced a functional and UX brief for the backoffice.
- Defined the main operational roles:
  - admin
  - sales
  - warehouse
  - quality
  - production
- Framed the backoffice around products, lots, stock, quality, orders, traceability, and auditability.

### Ecommerce

- Built a first ecommerce prototype.
- Refocused the shop on a tighter and more coherent almond assortment.
- Added format selection on product cards.
- Added bilingual structure (`IT` / `EN`).
- Added guest checkout plus optional account creation.
- Added business request flow.
- Centered the traceability concept through lot and QR storytelling.
- Reworked the visual direction toward a cleaner premium brand language.

### Visual Assets

- Added real orchard imagery supplied by the owner for a more credible brand presentation.
- Kept external imagery only where local assets do not yet cover derivatives or editorial placeholders.
